# Partner Success Metrics

## Key Metrics by Category

### Revenue Metrics

| Metric | Definition | Target |
|--------|------------|--------|
| Partner-sourced revenue | Revenue from partner deals | Growing 20% YoY |
| Partner-influenced revenue | Revenue with partner involvement | 30% of total |
| Revenue per partner | Average revenue per active partner | $100K+ |

### Partner Activity Metrics

| Metric | Definition | Target |
|--------|------------|--------|
| Certified partners | % with current certification | >80% |
| Deal registration | % deals registered | >50% |
| Active partners | Partners with revenue in 12 months | Growing |

### Customer Success Metrics

| Metric | Definition | Target |
|--------|------------|--------|
| Partner NPS | Partner satisfaction score | 50+ |
| Implementation success | % successful deployments | >90% |
| Support satisfaction | Partner support ratings | >4.0 |
