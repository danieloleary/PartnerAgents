## How to Use This Template

**Purpose:**
This program establishes standards for partner expertise and validates competency.

**Steps:**

1. **Define levels** — Bronze/Silver/Gold certification (1 hour)
2. **Create exams** — Develop exam content (2 hours)
3. **Build process** — Set up tracking and delivery (1 hour)
4. **Launch** — Roll out to partners (ongoing)

# Partner Certification Program

## Certification Levels

| Level | Focus | Requirements | Benefits |
|-------|-------|--------------|----------|
| **Foundation** | Product basics | Online exam | Deal registration |
| **Advanced** | Sales & delivery | Exam + practical | Co-marketing |
| **Expert** | Complex deals | Exam + case study | Priority support |

---

## Exam Structure

### Foundation Exam
- 30 multiple choice questions
- 60 minutes
- 70% passing score
- Online proctored

### Advanced Exam
- 50 questions + 2 case studies
- 90 minutes
- 80% passing score
- Live proctoring
